🎓 What Is PlayHive.Net?

A free collection of the best brain-training, skill-building, and creative games — built for kids, loved by everyone. Learning and fun go hand-in-hand here.

No accounts. No purchases. No data collection. Just games that make you think, laugh, and level up.

  • ✓100% free, no paywalls, ever
  • ✓Zero ads — not now, not ever
  • ✓No cookies or analytics
  • ✓No autoplay audio or pop-ups
  • ✓Open source & self-hostable

👪 For Parents

This site collects absolutely no personal data. No third-party scripts, no tracking pixels. Games run entirely in your browser.

  • ✓No personal data collected
  • ✓No third-party scripts or ad networks
  • ✓No links that navigate off-site
  • ✓Games run in secure sandboxed iframes
  • ✓Safe for all children

💖 100% Open Source

PlayHive is an open-source project hosted on GitHub! Our code is completely public, which means anyone can verify that the site is safe, ad-free, and exactly as we promise.
